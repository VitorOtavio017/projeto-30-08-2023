About Einstein:

Albert Einstein, 14/03/1879-18/April/1955, was born in the German Empire and died in the United States. He is known for discovering and answering many concepts and formulas such as; general relativity, special relativity and the unified field theory. During his life Einstein moved to many countries allowing him to contribute to many universites and intutions around the world. He married twice in his life but his first wife is the mother to all 3 of his children; Eduard Einstein, Hans Albert Einstein and Lieserl Einstein. Einstien's influences were Hendrik Lorentz and Hermann Minkowski, both were great in the fields of physics and mathematics.
